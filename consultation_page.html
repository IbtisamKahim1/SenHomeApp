About Our Consultations

Our consultation services are designed to support families and caregivers in providing the best care for individuals with sensory needs. We offer personalised advice tailored to your situation, covering topics like:

  • Home modification for sensory-friendly environments
  • Behavioral strategies for everyday challenges
  • Tools and resources for better engagement
  • Educational support and strategies

What to Expect

During a consultation, you will:

  • Speak with a trained specialist
  • Identify specific areas of concern
  • Receive actionable recommendations
  • Discuss follow-up support options
